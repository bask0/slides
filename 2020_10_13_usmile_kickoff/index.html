Differential programming with PyTorch

PyTorch does automatic differentiation: A recorder records what operations have performed, and then it replays it backward to compute the gradients. → Hybrid modeling out-of-the-box

→ PyTorch handles backpropagation with various gradient descent methdos. Nothing «special» about hybrid modeling (technically speaking).
